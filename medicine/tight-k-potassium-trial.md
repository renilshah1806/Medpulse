# TIGHT K Clinical Trial Information

## Essentials
- **Objective:** To assess the efficacy and safety of tight control of potassium levels in patients.
- **Design:** Randomized controlled trial.
- **Participants:** Patients with chronic kidney disease.

## Clinical Impact
- Improved management of potassium levels can lead to reduced morbidity and mortality in patients with kidney issues.
- May influence future clinical guidelines regarding potassium management.

## Exam Relevance
- Topics related to electrolyte imbalances, pharmacological interventions, and patient management are often tested in medical examinations, making this trial relevant for students preparing for board exams.
